User Story - Payment
=========================

**Story:** As a customer, I want multiple choice to pay on the website.

**Background/Business value:** We want to be able to provide costumers to choose that what they want to pay. Thus, we attract more customers to the website. 

**Details:** We provide multiple payment methods to the users, so they can choose how they want to pay(Bitcoin,credit card, cash etc.).

**Acceptance Criteria:**
<li>Multiple payment methods</li>
