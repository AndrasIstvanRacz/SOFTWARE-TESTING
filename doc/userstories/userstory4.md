User Story - Sale
=========================

**Story:** As a customer, I want to know there is a sale on the product.

**Background/Business value:** We will notify customers if any of the products are on sale. 

**Details:** We notify our customers of current discounts via email.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
<li>Send email to registered users about the sale</li>
<li>How long the sale is available</li>
<li>How much is the discount on the products</li>
